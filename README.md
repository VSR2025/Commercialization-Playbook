# Commercialization-Playbook
An interactive commercialization framework — field-tested across enterprise engagements — to help companies decide if, why, and how to bring an internal capability to market.

This playbook was built from real work — not theory. It has guided go and no-go decisions across enterprise clients in industries ranging from entertainment to infrastructure. 
The framework asks three questions in sequence: Can you productize this capability? Should you? And if so, how?

In the Gen AI era, every company thinks it has something worth bringing to market. Most are wrong. This tool helps you find out — before you commit resources, distract your team, or hand competitors the thing that makes you special.
